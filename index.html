Ancient Temple

Chennakeshava Temple,1250

The Chennakeshava temple, dedicated to the Hindu god Vishnu, is located in Aralaguppe, a small town in Karnataka state, India.The temple was built around 1250 during the rule of the Hoysala Empire King Vira Someshwara.The temple is a protected monument under the Karnataka state division of the Archaeological Survey of India.
